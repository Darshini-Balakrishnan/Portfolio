About Me

I am a Data Engineer and Data Analyst with 1+ years of experience designing scalable data platforms and transforming complex, large-scale datasets into actionable business insights across healthcare and financial domains.

My expertise spans data engineering, analytics engineering, and business intelligence, with hands-on experience building and optimizing ETL pipelines, data models, and cloud-based workflows that support reliable reporting, dashboards, and advanced analytics.

I am proficient in Python, SQL, Spark, and cloud technologies, and specialize in leveraging AWS (AWS Certified Data Engineer – Associate) and Databricks to deliver performant, maintainable, and analytics-ready data solutions.

I bridge the gap between engineering and analytics by ensuring high-quality data pipelines while applying machine learning, statistical analysis, and visualization tools such as Power BI and R to support data-driven decision-making.

I hold a Master’s degree in Data Analytics & Engineering (GPA: 3.96) from George Mason University and a B.Tech in Information Technology, combining strong academic foundations with hands-on industry and project experience.

My work includes building fraud detection pipelines, developing clinical question-answering systems using OpenAI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and optimizing large-scale data workflows for analytics and AI applications.

Beyond technical execution, I am passionate about emerging data technologies, scalable system design, and using data to solve real-world problems at scale.
