About Me

I am a results-driven Data Engineer and Analyst with two years of experience designing scalable data platforms and transforming complex datasets into actionable insights.

My expertise spans data engineering, advanced analytics, and cloud technologies, with proven success in building ETL pipelines, optimizing databases,and delivering interactive dashboards for healthcare and financial clients.

Proficient in Python, SQL, Spark, and modern cloud ecosystems, I specialize in leveraging AWS (AWS Certified Data Engineer Associate) and Databricks to develop robust data solutions.

I bridge the gap between engineering and analytics by ensuring reliable data pipelines while applying machine learning and visualization tools such as Power BI and R to drive decision-making.

With a Master’s in Data Analytics & Engineering (4.0 GPA) from George Mason University and a B.Tech in Information Technology, I combine academic excellence with hands-on industry experience.

My projects include building fraud detection pipelines, developing clinical QA systems using OpenAI and RAG, and optimizing large-scale data workflows.

Beyond coding, I am passionate about emerging technologies, open-source contributions, and using data to solve real-world challenges at scale.
